Contributions

We accept submissions for plenary talks and poster contributions. Submission of contributions will start as soon as the SOC finalizes the broad science themes for the meeting.

Our facilities can support about 500 attendees with talks or posters. Based on the previous Cool Stars meetings, we expect that every interested astronomer should have a chance to join in, but the only guarantee is to submit your abstract early. Subscribe to our announcement list to be informed when registration opens.
