Registration

Registration will open on March 1st, the registration fee will be about 500 $.

Our facilities can support about 500 attendees. Based on the previous Cool Stars meetings, we expect that every interested astronomer should have a chance to join in, but the only guarantee is to register early. Subscribe to our announcement list to be informed when registration opens.

Financial support

Limited financial support is available to allow students and other early career astronomers to attend CS20, if their institution cannot provide sufficient funds. The application form is available here. The deadline to submit the financial support application is March 7.
